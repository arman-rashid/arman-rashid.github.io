About me

After completing my master's from the University of Kashmir, I moved to Bangalore for my PhD studies. My research is revolving around molecular electronics. We are using a multidisciplinary approach to envisage the possibilities of using single or an ensemble of molecules to mimic the functionalities of various electronic devices. These possibilities represent the ultimate limit of miniaturization(below 1nm scale)of electronic components. This will allow us to carry forward the legacy of Moore's laws which is otherwise limited by the ultimate nature of matter at nanoscale-quantum mechanical effects and device physics.
Currently i am on a research employment in Physikalisches Institut (PHI), Karlsruhe Institute of Technology(KIT) where i am involved in building necessary instrumentation and exploring light-matter interactions in liq. He temperature Scanning tunnelling microscope(a magnificient beast i would say)
